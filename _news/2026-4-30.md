Four papers were accepted to ICML 2026, covering [self-supervised rewards for RL (SSL4RL)](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=vWNZT9waPN), [single-stage sparse multi-vector retrieval (SSR)](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=pp1enKGv2v), [agentic graph reasoning (GRASP)](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=nWOjSTq1ae) and [data scale-difficulty interplay](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=LCosvYhXvi). One latent visual reasoning work [Monet](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=S6AcSR6Mf1)  was accepted to CVPR 2026.
